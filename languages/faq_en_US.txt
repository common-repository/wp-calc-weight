<h2>How to install</h2>

<ol>
	<li>Click the bookmark "Plug-ins", then <strong>"download plug-in"</strong></li>
	<li>Select the "wp-calc-weight.zip", and click <strong>Install</strong></li>
	<li>After the installation is complete, click <strong>Activate Plugin</strong></li>
</ol>

<h2>How does this work</h2>

<ol>
	<li>Move to the section "Settings" and create the calculator that you need.</li>
	<li>2 Select short-code from "Settings" section and insert it on the page on which you want to display the calculator. For more information, see section "Short-Codes" below.</li>
</ol>


<h2>Short-codes</h2>

<p>
Examples of short-codes:
<br/>

[calc-weight]

<br/>
or 
<br/>

[calc-weight age=27]<br/>

or 
<br/>

[calc-weight gender=female]<br/>

or 
<br/>

[calc-weight age=27 gender=male]<br/>
</p>
